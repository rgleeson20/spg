About Sarah G. Leeson

Sarah G. Leeson is a contemporary artist specializing in mixed-media abstractions. Born in 1990 in Bristol, she pursued Fine Arts at the University of Cambridge and has exhibited across Europe since 2015.

Her work explores the interplay of color, form, and texture, highlighting the relationship between urban environments and natural patterns. When she’s not in the studio, Sarah volunteers as a community muralist and leads workshops for emerging young artists.

Sarah’s main mediums include acrylic, collage, and digital illustration. She draws inspiration from cityscapes and natural landscapes, bridging them through vibrant textures and layered compositions.
